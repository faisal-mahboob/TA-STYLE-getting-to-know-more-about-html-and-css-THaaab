How to make Biryani

sheep Curry Chai

Gather the ingredients

Get rice 8kg, one whole chicken, vegetable oil, garlic, onion, cardamom, ginger paste, shan biryani spice pack, salt, chilli powder, turmeric

Cooking Instructions

Wash the rice, then put water in the rice boil. Until rice is 25% cooked. Put chicken in the cooking pot and and put all spices and vegetable,cook until chicken is done. Out rice on top of the chicken and cover dish for 40 mins.
